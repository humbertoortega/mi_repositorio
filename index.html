ABOUT ME

Taylhardat, is the name of the electronic music project created by the Venezuelan composer and producer Humberto Ortega. Inside the wide range of the electronic genre, his musical styles include that of Ambient music, World music, techno and soundtrack.

Taylhardat's style is characterized by all the instrumental styles and an original mix of electronic, Techno, Ethnic and chillout music. These instrumental melodies are constructed over solid base tones,and pads with attractive rhythms.

His music uses electronic synthesizers, samplers, bringing interesting sound effects that mix with the main music. However, several songs showcase classical instruments, including Pianos, Guitars, Cellos, Violins, etc.

After years of inactivity, forced upon him by the country's situation, he migrates to Argentina in 2018, where he starts to study electronic music production. By the end of 2019 he started his own composition and the creation of his musical project " Taylhardat".

By 2020, released on all digital platforms, he independently debuts his album, "The Story Begins.."
